Both are a community gathering spot

The 1908 building that is home to the Riverview Cafe and Wine Bar has been a neighborhood destination for over a century. At one point the space was the home of a soda fountain, and it was the place to take a date, bring the family on a lazy weekend afternoon, and catch up on the latest happenings around town.
When we opened the Riverview Cafe in 1998, we wanted the building to once again be the hub of community life and a local destination and be: - a place for neighbors to gather over a cup of coffee or glass of wine - a place where parents and kids would be welcome - a great place for a romantic date night.
More than fifteen years later we've seen first dates, babies grow up, and neighbors become like family.
